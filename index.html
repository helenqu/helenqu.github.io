RESUME

PENNAPPS
I'm the Vice President of the PennApps Sponsorship committee. PennApps was the nation's first college hackathon and remains, at least to me, its best. In order to pull it off, we bombard hundreds of companies with emails in order to raise over $325,000 in corporate sponsorships every semester.
THE DINING PHILOSOPHERS
I'm also a member of the Hacking/Learning committee of The Dining Philosophers, Penn's computer science student organization. We plan to organize several activities this year, including Code Weekend and Tech Talks, in order to promote hacking on campus as well as offer opportunities to attend hackathons at other schools.
PENNSUSTAINS
I'm the director of logistics for PennSustains, a campus-wide sustainability design competition. We want to encourage students to engineer solutions to real problems both on Penn's campus and in the city of Philadelphia as a whole. Winning teams are awarded prizes totaling over $7,000 to actually implement their ideas.
